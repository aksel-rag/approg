Welcome to Duck World!

Begin by creating a duck, naming it, and starting to play. Your duck will have four attributes:

Happiness
Cleanliness
Hunger
Sleepyness

The duck's happiness will decrease and increase based on its other main attributes.

If the duck's hunger falls below 35, it will lose 33% of its happiness.

If the duck becomes tired and reaches the "sleepy" stage, it will again lose 33% of its happiness.

If the duck becomes dirty, you will see its appearance and mood change, and this will also cause its happiness to be reduced.

If these three stats fall below the thresholds, the duck will die of boredom.


Your duck's attributes such as sleep and cleanliness decrease every 10 seconds, and hunger is reduced every 3 seconds.



Dev Options

Enabling dev options allows you to reduce the hunger, cleanliness, and sleep status manually.
